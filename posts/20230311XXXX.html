    英语六级翻译真题2

    • 翻译1

    延安位于陕西省北部，地处黄河中游，是中国革命的圣地。
    Located in the northern part of Shaanxi Provincen and in the middle reaches of the Yellow River，Yan’an is the sacred land of the Chinese Revolution.

    毛泽东等老一辈革命家曾在这里生活战斗了十三个春秋，领导了抗日战争和解放战争，培育了延安精神，为中国革命做出了巨大贡献。
    The old generation of revolutionaries such as Mao Zedong lived and fought here for thirteen years, leading the War of Resistance against Japanese Aggression and the War of Liberation, cultivating the spirit of Yan’an, and making great contribution to the Chinese revolution.

    延安的革命旧址全国数量最大，分布最广，级别最高。
    With the largest number of revolutionary sites that are distributed widest and have the highest level in the country，

    延安是全国爱国主义，革命传统和延安精神教育基地。
    Yan’an is a national base for patriotism, revolutionary tradition and spiritual education.

    延安有9个革命纪念馆，珍藏着中共中央和老一辈革命家在延安时期留存下来的大量重要物品，因此享有中国革命博物馆城的美誉。
    There are nine revolutionary memorial halls, which hold a large number of important items left by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the old generation of revolutionaries during the Yan’an period, so it enjoys the reputation of the museum city of Chinese revolution.
